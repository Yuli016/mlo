This website is my Individual Learning Plan Portfolio of my courses and how I have fullfilled my each Communication Design Major Learning Outcome, also providing a copy of my ILP Excel Sheet, and Courses description and outcomes. I'm in the School of Computing and Design (SCD) and it educates students to apply state-of-the-art information technology to solve real world communication problems in a socially responsible way. In addition to mastering the technical knowledge and skills, graduates of SCD programs excel in communication, collaboration and innovative problem solving.


My degree is Communication Design BS and it provides a broad-based understanding of current technologies and the application of media arts, web design, animation design and game design. Students develop technological knowledge combined with design, communication and management skills. Graduates of the program are qualified for a range of careers in the design and high-tech industries.


My concentration is Game Design & Interactive Media that prepares students to work in teams, communicating and collaborating with other designers, artists and engineers in creating and publishing games. The concentration courses are Game Design and Interactive Media I, Environment Modeling, Character Design, Level Design, Character Animation, and Game Design and Interactive Media II.
